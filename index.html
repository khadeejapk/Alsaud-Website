About Us

Al Saud Advanced Coating Systems (ASACS) is a specialized industrial coating company serving critical sectors including Oil & Gas, Petrochemicals, Automotive, HVAC, and Heavy Engineering across Saudi Arabia and the GCC.

Established to meet the region’s growing demand for high-performance surface protection, ASACS has become a trusted partner for advanced coating solutions - offering a full suite of services from anti-corrosion and anti-friction treatments to high-temperature and non-stick coatings.

Our facilities, strategically located in Al Khobar (KSA), Umm Al Quwain (UAE), and Birkat Al Awamer (Qatar), are equipped to handle precision components and large industrial assemblies alike, ensuring consistent quality and reliability.

With a strong foundation in innovation, engineering expertise, and international certifications (ISO 9001, 14001, 45001), Al Saud Advanced Coating Systems is committed to protecting critical assets, reducing maintenance costs, and enhancing equipment performance in the world’s most demanding industrial environments.
